# Verklýsing - Net Worth & Savings Calculator

A clean, elegant, modern website that helps people understand how everyday financial decisions affect their long-term net worth.

## Features

### 🧮 Net Worth Calculator
- **Assets tracking**: Cash/savings, investments, retirement accounts, real estate, other assets
- **Liabilities tracking**: Debts, mortgage, credit cards, loans
- **Real-time calculation**: Instant net worth updates as you input values
- **Visual feedback**: Color-coded results (positive/negative net worth)

### 📈 Future Projections
- **Compound growth visualization**: See how your net worth could grow over 10, 20, 30, and 50 years
- **Customizable parameters**: Adjust monthly investment amount and expected annual return
- **Historical performance context**: Based on historical market performance data

### 🎯 Habit Impact Analyzer
The key feature that sets this calculator apart - visualizing the **true cost of everyday spending habits**:

- **Habit cost tracking**: Input daily, weekly, or monthly expenses (coffee, cigarettes, subscriptions)
- **Opportunity cost calculation**: Shows what that money could become if invested instead
- **Long-term impact**: Compare direct costs vs. investment potential over 1, 10, and 30 years
- **Visual comparison**: Side-by-side cards showing "Cost of Habit" vs "If Invested Instead"

## Key Questions Answered

✅ **What is my net worth today?**
✅ **How could my net worth grow over 10, 20, 30, or 50 years?**
✅ **What happens if I invest in different types of savings or asset vehicles?**
✅ **What is the long-term effect of one repeated spending habit if I redirect that money instead?**

## Design Philosophy

- **Clean and modern**: Minimalist interface focusing on clarity over complexity
- **Motivating and eye-opening**: Visual design that makes the impact of financial decisions emotionally compelling
- **Mobile-responsive**: Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Instant feedback**: Real-time calculations as you type

## Technical Implementation

- **Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript**: No frameworks or dependencies
- **Responsive design**: CSS Grid and Flexbox for optimal layouts
- **Modern styling**: CSS backdrop-filter effects, gradients, and smooth animations
- **Accessible**: Semantic HTML and proper form labels
- **Performance-optimized**: Lightweight and fast loading

## The Power of Compound Interest Visualization

This calculator makes abstract financial concepts tangible by showing:

- How a $5 daily coffee habit costs $1,825/year but could become $87,000+ over 30 years if invested
- How small, consistent investments compound exponentially over time
- The dramatic difference between spending and investing the same amount of money

## Usage

1. **Enter your current financial situation** in the Net Worth Calculator
2. **Set your investment goals** in the Future Projections section
3. **Analyze a spending habit** in the Habit Impact Analyzer to see its true long-term cost
4. **Compare scenarios** to make informed financial decisions
